Driving
The village of Welschneudorf is about 1 hour’s drive from both Frankfurt airport and Cologne/Bonn airport.

By train
From Frankfurt airport train station (Frankfurt Flughafen), there is a direct train service to the nearest town of Montabaur (ICE-Bahnhof Montabaur), which takes approximately 30 minutes. From Cologne/Bonn airport train station (Köln/Bonn Flughafen), there is also a train service to Montabaur, which takes approximately 1 hour and includes one change. Please visit bahn.de for train times and bookings.
Montabaur train station is about 10 minutes’ drive from the wedding venue. We can assist with booking taxis from the train station nearer the time if required.
We are anticipating that most guests will arrive on Friday afternoon or evening.

Food will be provided on the farm in the Gutshaus restaurant in the evening. As guests will be arriving at different times, there won’t be a set time for food. We will be around and everyone can arrive in their own time and join us to eat whenever it suits.
